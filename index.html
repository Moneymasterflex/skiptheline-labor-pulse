The Truth Behind the Numbers

7.3 million people are unemployed.
Most never got a real conversation.

Look at this map. Every red pin is a state where unemployment is above 4.5%. But here's what the data doesn't show most of those people applied. They submitted resumes. They waited. They never heard back.

Not because they weren't qualified. Because they never got in the room.

Automated systems reject 75% of resumes before a human ever sees them. Ghost job postings collect applications that go nowhere. Referrals and insider networks determine who actually gets hired and most people don't have one.

That's the line. And we're done waiting in it.

SkipTheLine connects job seekers directly with hiring managers before the application, before the ATS, before the black hole. We put the human back in hiring. Talk first. Resume second. Hired faster.
